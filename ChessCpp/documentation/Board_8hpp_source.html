68  /*
69  Places the pieces on the board following a custom configuration.
70 
71  When a piece is initialised in a non-standard location, it is treated as
72  if it had moved there. So, for instance, castling rights would not apply
73  to a king initialised in A3. Furthermore, en passant rights are not given
74  upon initialisation, meaning you cannot directly initialise two pawns for
75  en passant and execute it the next turn.
76  Finally, the board defaults to Zobrist hashing for the 3-fold and 5-fold
77  repetition rules.
78 
79  Throws in case of:
80  1) invalid coordinates;
81  2) multiple pieces sharing the same coordinates;
82  3) multiple promotions (e.g. two white pawns in the last row).
83  */
