◆ isMovePlausible()

bool Chess::Piece::isMovePlausible ( Coordinates const &  source,
Coordinates const &  destination 
) const

Verifies if the piece would be able to move from the source to the destination according to its internal logic. Checks or castling are considered board-level moves, and are not taken into account.

Performs integrity checks shared among all pieces. For example: coordinates must be within limits, a piece cannot move to a square taken by an ally and source and destination cannot match.

Returns the derived class' isMovePlausibleSpecific() to check the logic of each piece.
